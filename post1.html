Introduction

Composting is a natural process that transforms organic waste into nutrient-rich soil. Here are five benefits of composting for your garden:

1. Enriches Soil

Compost adds essential nutrients to the soil, improving its structure and fertility.

2. Reduces Waste

Composting reduces the amount of waste sent to landfills, helping the environment.

3. Saves Money

Using compost reduces the need for chemical fertilizers, saving you money.

4. Encourages Healthy Plants

Compost helps plants grow stronger and healthier by providing a balanced nutrient supply.

5. Supports Wildlife

Composting creates a habitat for beneficial microorganisms and insects that aid in plant growth.

Conclusion

Composting is a simple yet effective way to enhance your garden while contributing positively to the environment.
